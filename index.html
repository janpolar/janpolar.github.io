Hamzahthefantastic

About him

Hamzahthefantastic is a Yemeni-born comedy content creator and TikTok personality, known for his point-of-view style videos. With over 1.5 million fans on TikTok, he has become a significant online figure.One of his earliest videos went viral, kickstarting his rise to fame. In addition to his TikTok success, he launched a YouTube channel in January 2020, though by May of that year, he had yet to publish any content.

Childhood

Born in Yemen, he moved to the United States as a baby, relocating to Canada at the age of 17. His younger brother has made appearances in his TikTok videos. A notable moment in his career includes a comedic video posted on December 26, 2019, where he imitated Jacob Sartorius.

Fame

Hamzahthefantastic is also active on Twitch, streaming on the popular 4FreakShow channel alongside other creators such as Chase Rutherford, yodelinghaley, and Claire Drake. He co-hosts the podcast and YouTube show Out of Character with thatmartinkid.

Present

His blend of humor, creativity, and collaboration has made Hamzahthefantastic a recognizable name across multiple platforms. His youtube channel Slushy Noobz has accumilated 476K subscribers over the course of a year and a half. Him and his bestfriend martinthatkid have a successful youtube channel with thousands of loyal fans that admire them.
